About Delta Fitness Centre

Delta Fitness Centre is a dedicated and professionally managed gym located at Karimkulam, Elavanchery, Palakkad, Kerala. Our gym is designed for both ladies and gents who are serious about fitness, strength, and healthy living.

We believe that fitness is not just about lifting weights, but about proper guidance, discipline, and continuous motivation.

The gym is owned and operated by professional trainers Sujith and Rahul. Coach Sujith is also an experienced bodybuilder.
